# Attendee

Welcome to the world of events on ECTickets! Whether you're chasing the thrill of live music, or exploring workshops, our platform makes discovering, buying, and enjoying tickets effortless. As an attendee, you'll find everything from quick purchases to seamless check-ins designed with you in mind.

## Overview

ECTickets empowers you to focus on the experience, not the hassle. Our attendee-friendly tools include mobile-optimized browsing, secure payments, and real-time updatesâ€”all backed by robust support to ensure your events go smoothly.

### Key Features for Attendees

- **Seamless Purchasing**: One-click buys, promo code support, and flexible payment options (cards, wallets).
- **Event Start Notifications**: Get reminders, venue details, and last-minute updates via email.
- **Event Day Essentials**: QR code scanning for contactless entry, digital ticket wallets, and on-site support.
- **Buyer Protection**: Guaranteed refunds for canceled events and fraud monitoring for peace of mind.
- **Event Discovery (WIP)**: Personalized recommendations, advanced search filters (by location, genre, date), and interactive maps to find events near you.
